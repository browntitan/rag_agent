# Acme Agent Platform — Pricing & Plans

**Document status:** Active

## 1. Plan overview

AAP is available in three plans:

- **Free** — personal exploration
- **Pro** — small teams
- **Enterprise** — large orgs, SSO, compliance

## 2. Free plan

- Max workspaces: 1
- Max users: 2
- Daily tool calls: 200
- Retention: 7 days
- Support: community

## 3. Pro plan

- Max workspaces: 5
- Max users: 25
- Daily tool calls: 10,000
- Retention: 30 days
- Support: email support (business hours)

## 4. Enterprise plan

- Unlimited workspaces
- Unlimited users
- Daily tool calls: negotiable
- Retention: configurable (default 365 days)
- Support: 24/7 + designated TAM

### 4.1 Enterprise retention policy

Enterprise retention is **configurable**.

Default settings:

- **Trace retention:** 365 days
- **Conversation retention:** 365 days
- **Document upload retention:** 365 days

Optional settings:

- 30 days (minimum)
- 90 days
- 180 days
- 365 days
- custom (contractual)

## 5. Add-ons

- **Advanced Observability Pack**: extended traces, custom dashboards
- **Bring-your-own-key (BYOK)**: customer-managed encryption keys
- **Data Residency**: pin storage to a region

## 7. Common sales FAQs

### Q: Do tool calls count across all agents?
Yes. Tool calls are counted per workspace across all agents.

### Q: Do retrieval calls count as tool calls?
If retrieval is implemented as a tool, it counts as a tool call.

### Q: Does the Enterprise plan support SSO?
Yes, Enterprise supports SAML/OIDC SSO and SCIM provisioning.
