# Vendor Security Schedule

Document Type: requirements_doc  
Version: 3.1  
Owner: Third-Party Risk and Security Assurance

## 1. Security Baseline

This schedule defines minimum vendor security controls required for onboarding, continued operation, and annual reassessment.
All security controls in this schedule must be contractually enforceable and evidence-backed.
REQ-1101 Vendors must complete security baseline attestation before production onboarding.

Section 2: Vendor Security Control 2
2.1. Mandatory Control.
The vendor shall implement Vendor Security Control 2 in production environments.
REQ-1121 Vendor must provide evidence artifacts for Vendor Security Control 2 at onboarding and annually.
2.2. Oversight Mechanism.
The Customer shall verify Vendor Security Control 2 through control testing and evidence review.
REQ-1122 Vendor must remediate critical findings from Vendor Security Control 2 within 30 days.
2.3. Enforcement Requirement.
Any deficiency in Vendor Security Control 2 shall trigger formal risk acceptance or corrective action.
REQ-1123 Vendor must submit corrective action plan with milestones and owners.

Section 5: Encryption and Key Management
5.1. Data-at-Rest Encryption.
The vendor shall encrypt sensitive customer data at rest using industry-approved algorithms.
REQ-1151 Vendor must enforce AES-256 or stronger encryption for production storage systems.
5.2. Data-in-Transit Encryption.
The vendor shall protect data in transit using modern TLS configurations.
REQ-1152 Vendor must disable weak ciphers and deprecated protocols.
5.3. Key Lifecycle.
The vendor shall maintain formal key lifecycle procedures.
REQ-1153 Vendor must rotate key-encryption keys every 90 days and document rotation evidence.

Section 12: Identity, Access, and Privileged Controls
12.1. Identity Assurance.
The vendor shall enforce unique identities and strong authentication.
REQ-11121 Vendor must require MFA for privileged access across production and administrative surfaces.
12.2. Privileged Session Controls.
The vendor shall implement controls for privileged session monitoring and timeout.
REQ-11122 Vendor must configure privileged session timeout at 15 minutes of inactivity.
12.3. Revocation Timeliness.
The vendor shall revoke credentials quickly following role change or termination.
REQ-11123 Vendor must revoke privileged access within one hour of status change.

Section 18: Incident Notification and Cooperation
18.1. Initial Notification.
The vendor shall notify the Customer promptly after confirming a security incident affecting customer data.
REQ-11181 Vendor must issue initial notification within 12 hours of confirmation.
18.2. Status Updates.
The vendor shall provide recurring updates during incident response.
REQ-11182 Vendor must provide status updates every 30 minutes for severe incidents and every 2 hours for moderate incidents.
18.3. Closure Reporting.
The vendor shall provide a post-incident closure package.
REQ-11183 Vendor must include root cause, containment, and corrective plan in closure report.

Section 24: Assurance and Evidence Delivery
24.1. Audit Support.
The vendor shall support reasonable audits and control walkthroughs.
REQ-11241 Vendor must provide requested security artifacts within 10 business days.
24.2. Continuous Assurance.
The vendor shall provide periodic assurance evidence on key controls.
REQ-11242 Vendor must deliver quarterly assurance packet covering encryption, access, and incident controls.
24.3. Exception Governance.
The vendor shall track approved control exceptions and compensating controls.
REQ-11243 Vendor must document exception expiry dates and reapproval decisions.

Section 27: Termination Security
27.1. Offboarding Controls.
The vendor shall execute secure offboarding controls at contract termination.
REQ-11271 Vendor must remove customer data from active systems within 14 days unless legal hold applies.
27.2. Residual Risk Disclosure.
The vendor shall disclose residual risk for retained data under legal hold.
REQ-11272 Vendor must provide legal basis and expected retention horizon for retained records.
27.3. Final Attestation.
The vendor shall provide final termination attestation.
REQ-11273 Vendor must deliver signed termination security attestation within 21 days.
