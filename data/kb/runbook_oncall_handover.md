# Runbook — On-call Handover

**Owner:** SRE
**Last reviewed:** 2025-10-10

## 1. Handover goals

- Incoming on-call understands current state
- No open incidents are missed
- Ongoing investigations are documented

## 2. Handover checklist

1) Review open incidents and follow-ups
2) Review current alerts and noisy monitors
3) Review deployments in progress
4) Review recent changes (24h)
5) Confirm paging rotation and contacts

## 4. Common pitfalls

- Not transferring context for flaky alarms
- Not documenting partial mitigations
- Not communicating known customer escalations
