# Data Processing Addendum (Global)

Document Type: policy_doc  
Effective Date: 2026-02-15  
Applies To: Customer Personal Data Processing across EEA, UK, US, and APAC operations

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This addendum defines processing obligations for personal data handled by the Provider under the Master Services Agreement.
The Provider shall process personal data only on documented Customer instructions unless required by applicable law.
REQ-801 The Provider must maintain a processing instruction register for each customer tenant.

Clause 2: Data Governance Control 2
2.1. Processing Guardrail.
The Provider shall enforce purpose limitation controls for Data Governance Control 2.
REQ-821 The Provider must validate lawful basis and instruction scope before activation of Data Governance Control 2.
2.2. Control Operation.
The Provider shall maintain auditable control execution records for Data Governance Control 2.
REQ-822 The Provider must provide control execution evidence within 72 hours of Customer request.
2.3. Breach and Exception Handling.
The Provider shall classify and escalate exceptions associated with Data Governance Control 2.
REQ-823 The Provider must document corrective actions and closure date for each exception.

Clause 9: Cross-Border Transfer Controls
9.1. Transfer Mechanism.
The Provider shall not transfer customer personal data outside approved jurisdictions without valid legal transfer safeguards.
REQ-891 The Provider must maintain transfer impact assessments for each restricted transfer route.
9.2. Subprocessor Location Registry.
The Provider shall maintain a current list of processing locations used by subprocessors.
REQ-892 The Provider must update the location registry within five business days of infrastructure change.
9.3. Data Residency Routing.
The Provider shall apply geo-fencing controls for residency-constrained tenants.
REQ-893 The Provider must block cross-region replication for residency-constrained datasets unless approved by Customer.

Clause 14: Deletion and Return
14.1. Termination Deletion.
The Provider shall delete personal data within 14 calendar days of valid termination instruction unless legally required otherwise.
REQ-8141 The Provider must provide deletion proof artifacts and retained-data legal basis summary.
14.2. Return Format.
The Provider shall provide data export in structured machine-readable format before deletion when requested.
REQ-8142 The Provider must complete export preparation within seven business days.
14.3. Backup Treatment.
The Provider shall isolate and age out backup copies according to backup retention policy.
REQ-8143 The Provider must ensure backups containing customer personal data expire within 21 days.

Clause 18: Incident Notification
18.1. Initial Notice.
The Provider shall notify the Customer of confirmed personal data incidents without undue delay.
REQ-8181 The Provider must issue initial notice within 12 hours of confirmation.
18.2. Ongoing Updates.
The Provider shall provide periodic updates until incident closure.
REQ-8182 The Provider must provide update cadence every 30 minutes for severe incidents and every 2 hours for moderate incidents.
18.3. Final Report.
The Provider shall issue a closure report with remedial actions.
REQ-8183 The Provider must provide closure report within five business days of incident containment.

Clause 25: Audit and Demonstrability
25.1. Audit Support.
The Provider shall support reasonable audits related to data processing controls.
REQ-8251 The Provider must provide requested artifacts within 10 business days.
25.2. Demonstrability.
The Provider shall maintain evidence that controls are not only designed but operational.
REQ-8252 The Provider must maintain monthly demonstrability packs for high-risk processing activities.

Clause 27: Regulatory Cooperation
27.1. Authority Requests.
The Provider shall promptly notify Customer of regulatory requests unless prohibited by law.
REQ-8271 The Provider must notify within 24 hours when legally permitted.
27.2. Response Drafting.
The Provider shall support Customer in drafting regulator responses involving shared processing contexts.
REQ-8272 The Provider must provide technical and control evidence needed for regulator response preparation.
